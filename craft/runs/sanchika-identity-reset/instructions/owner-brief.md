# Owner brief — Sanchika identity reset

## Outcome

Sanchika must feel like a reproducible design language with a genuine soul:
beautiful, memorable, crafted, artistic, trustworthy, lively, and emotionally
immediate. It must help humans and AI create serious interfaces with coherent
character, rather than merely presenting a safe component catalogue.

## Product truth

Sanchika remains a contract-first design system and SDK surface. It makes
reusable interface decisions portable across products while keeping runtime,
data, auth, and release boundaries separate. It is not an AI runtime, an
automated compliance judge, a customer-proof surface, or a product-app shell.

## Narrative premise

The semantic anchor is Sanchika / संचिका as collection or compilation. The
identity should make distinct parts feel gathered, separated, and reconciled
without flattening their character. The Witness Joint remains an open interval
between source, interpretation, and accountable human decision; it is identity
geometry, never simulated product evidence.

## Required emotional effect

The landing should create an instant sense that the system was made with care
and has a point of view. Reproducibility for coding agents should read as a
creative capability: a living grammar through which people and AI can make
interfaces that are clear, humane, and recognisably crafted.

## Explicit rejections

- generic SaaS, generic AI startup, or generic design-tool visual language;
- equal-card grids, bento layouts, empty heroic whitespace, or token catalogues
  presented as a brand;
- fake dashboards, fake documents, fake authority, review rings, KPI panels,
  or claims standing in for proof;
- a generic `S` logo, a coloured-square monogram, or borrowed compliance
  iconography;
- purple AI gradients, glassmorphism, ledger styling, serif theatre, or
  decorative motion without meaning;
- visual directions that differ only by palette, density, or typographic scale.

## Acceptance criteria for exploration

Each territory must have a distinct central metaphor, composition, typographic
voice, and identity mechanism. It must reserve a real rendered HTML proof
surface for later exploration, keep source, interpretation, and human
accountability visibly distinct, and remain possible to render without raster
assets or motion. No direction is production-approved by this brief.
