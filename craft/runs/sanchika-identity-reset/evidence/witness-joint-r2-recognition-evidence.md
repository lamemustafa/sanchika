# Witness Joint R2 recognition proxy — 2026-07-23

Two 1440px browser viewports were rendered from the rebuilt Witness Joint
markup and its self-hosted Anek-controlled stylesheet. The blind transforms
were applied to the parsed built HTML before the page was written for capture;
they preserve the original DOM geometry and CSS rather than blur or flatten a
reference image.

- `witness-joint-r2-semantic-blind.webp` (12,420 bytes): all visible copy is
  deterministic pseudocopy except the Sanchika wordmark.
- `witness-joint-r2-identity-blind.webp` (12,776 bytes): all visible copy is
  deterministic pseudocopy and the wordmark is replaced with the eight-letter
  pseudoword `VELORIAN`.

Three fresh, independent AI matchers compared the blind boards with the three
R2 direction boards. Each recognised both variants as Witness Joint from the
asymmetric headline-to-grid relation, three-bay open frame, narrow central
joint, connector marker, and lower specimen-panel placement. No matcher used
colour as its decisive cue: semantic-blind **3/3**, identity-blind **3/3**,
colour-only **false**.

This reaches the recognition-proxy threshold only. It is AI recognition-proxy
evidence, not user validation, and it does not clear the separate keyboard,
forced-colors, reduced-motion, or owner gates.
